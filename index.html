About Carol

Since she was a child, Carol has enjoyed making things, particularly brightly-colored figurines out of the plastic clay Sculpey. As an adult, she discovered the meditative qualities of cross-stitch and crocheting. She has also tried her hand at designing her own Halloween costumes.

This site showcases a few of her creations. Please enjoy!
