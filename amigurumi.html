Baby-safe Substitutions

Not all crochet dolls are safe for children under 3 due to choking hazards. Here are some common dangers and the baby-safe options.

Hazard Solution
safety eyes felt or embroidered eyes
buttons crochet button
removable hat hat stitched to the head
